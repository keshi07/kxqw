Inspection Process

At AZoom Car Rental, we prioritize the quality and safety of our cars. After you return the vehicle, our employees will perform a detailed inspection to ensure everything is in proper condition.

Inspection Checklist

  • Exterior inspection for any visible damages (scratches, dents, etc.).
  • Interior cleanliness and condition.
  • Battery charge level and mileage.
  • Functionality of key components (e.g., lights, brakes, wipers).

Billing Process

Once the inspection is complete, the final bill will be calculated. The total amount includes:

  • Rental cost based on the reserved period (daily, weekly, or monthly).
  • Additional charges for any damages or policy violations.
  • Taxes and other applicable fees.

Payments can only be made via the credit card used during the reservation. A receipt will be emailed to you once the payment is processed.

Note:

Please ensure the car is returned on time and in good condition to avoid additional charges.
